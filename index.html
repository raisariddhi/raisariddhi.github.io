GigTable

GigTable is a conceptual platform designed to simplify gig organization, communication, and task management for local musicians and bands. Explore the ideation, model analysis, prototyping, final design, and evaluation phases as we endeavor to improve the gig management experience.

OurHour

OurHour is the first office hours queue management website for the CS department at UW-Madison. Its simplistic design aims to solve one pressing issue: maintaining a queue of students who seek help for office hours and to make it easier for TAs to help and mark off students.

Enigma Machine

The Enigma machine, known for its complexity and role in cryptography history, is simulated here for educational and illustrative purposes. It is made with C and demonstrates the basic principles of the Enigma machine, allowing users to experiment with different rotor configurations, rotations, and encryption/decryption processes.

EasyMeals

EasyMeals is a mobile app designed to simplify meal planning and cooking for college students living in on-campus apartments. This app design includes features like a customizable meal calendar, grocery list management, and an ingredient-based recipe search to help students save time and enjoy home-cooked meals. Developed with user insights and feedback.
